Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the app work offline?
A: Yes. After the initial download, all disposal information is stored on your device.

Q: Is my data collected?
A: No. The app does not collect any personal information. See our Privacy Policy.

Q: How do I search for an item?
A: Tap "Search & Browse" and type the item name. Results appear as you type.

Q: What if I can't find my item?
A: Try the "Guided Search" feature which asks questions to help identify the correct disposal method. You can also browse by category.

Q: Is this app only for Old Lyme residents?
A: The app is designed for Old Lyme, CT residents. Some information (like transfer station locations) is specific to Old Lyme.

About

Waste and Recycling Guide was developed for the Town of Old Lyme Waste & Recycling Committee to help residents properly dispose of household items.
